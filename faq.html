Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



  1. What is the Objective of this project ?

    The Aim of this research is to develop a Smart Building prototype in real time operational environment at by applying Complex Event Processing and Analytics to perform data aggregation, co-relation, learning and temporal analysis for the optimized building operations as shown in the modules section of this website.

  2. What are the Problems intended to be addressed by proposed project ?

    Presently buildings are equipped with lighting, heating, air conditioning, elevator control, security systems along with fire and smoke detection and other necessities. All these systems are manual driven and operating in silos at the cost of building spaces, resources, energy and cost effectiveness. Smart buildings promise to enhance efficiency, through interconnection of all these systems in a cost effective way.

  3. Who has identified the problem and its relevance to the objectives of ICPS ?

    Development of Smart Grids and Smart Cities are the two highly motivated ongoing projects of Government of India. Both of these projects are facing difficulty to adjust with the existing infrastructure and significant upgradation of existing infrastructure is required to cope up these projects.

  4. What are the likely impact foreseen in the ICPS subject area or related areas?

    In future smart buildings will be in a high demand to cater smart grids which are aiming to establish energy-efficient infrastructure thus as an outcome of proposed research a building could quickly able to regulate temperature, illumination, ventilation and audio visual needs in accordance with the occupancy patterns

  5. What is the relevance of the project to the work already going on in the organisation?

    IIIT Allahabad has recently installed a roof top solar power generation plant on the roof of boys hostel to generate 300 KW electricity. Nearly it is also planned to install another 300 KW rooftop plant along with 940 solar panels and 10 invertors and connect it to the power grid. The generated solar energy could also be integrated in the vision of smart building and an energy efficient environment could be established

  6. What is the abstract of the project?

    Smart buildings equipped with sensors and electronic devices as a Cyber Physical Systems (CPS) offer great research perspective to explore communication, computation and controlling of physical devices by using real time Complex Event Processing (CEP) and analytics. In the present research, a model for real time CEP and analytics have been proposed for Smart-Building at IIIT Allahabad, India.

    The research will also highlight the strength, weakness, and challenges addressed in the proposed system.

  7. What are the Implementation arrangements proposed for the project ?

    The project prototype will be initially implemented and tested at IIIT Allahabad. Later it can be to deployed to any similar organization as per the specific needs of the system. A big data analytics lab is already set up under the supervision of project investigator to facilitate hardware and software platforms for data analytics and complex event processing.

    The project investigator is having a research group of 3 Ph.D. Scholars, 4 post graduate students and approximately 10 teams of under graduate students and all are enthusiastic to be the part of this proposed research proposal.
